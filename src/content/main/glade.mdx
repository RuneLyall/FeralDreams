Welcome traveler, you who come seeking a safe space from the rest of the world, a place where calm winds and quiet waters offer sanctuary. This is The Glade, a quiet but welcoming space, where you can just be yourself, explore, take time away from it all. Sometimes things happen here that we least expect, we meet those we didn't know we could or we just find out things about ourselves that we didn't realize we didn't know. For now I bid you, enjoy your stay, and after a spell decide if you want to return.

There is nothing here other than the wind for the moment, the rest shall come in time, be patient, or don't, in the end the decision is yours. If this is all too much, or you just need to get away then please [Exit](https://google.ca) step through this gateway.
